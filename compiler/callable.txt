-- FUNCTIONS -- 
sqrt(a) : the square root of a
exp(a) : e^a
ln(a) : natural log of a
log(b,a) : log base b of a
sin(a) : sine of angle a given in radians
cos(a) : cosine of angle a given in radians
tan(a) : tangent of angle a given in radians
cot(a) : cotangent of angle a given in radians
sec(a) : secant of angle a given in radians
csc(a) : cosecant of angle a given in radians
arcsin(a) : arcsine of a in radians
arccos(a) : arccosine of a in radians
arctan(a) : arctangent of a in radians
arccot(a) : arccotangent of a in radians
arcsec(a) : arcsecant of a in radians
arccsc(a) : arccosecant of a in radians

-- CONSTANTS --
e : euler's constant (2.71...)
pi : circle circumference over diameter (3.14...)
tau : circle circumference over radius (6.28...)
